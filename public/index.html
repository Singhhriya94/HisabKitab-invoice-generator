Frequently Asked Questions

What is Hisab Kitab?

Hisab Kitab is an invoice generator website that helps users create, preview, save, edit, and download invoices easily.

Do I need to sign up before using the invoice generator?

Yes, users need to sign up first and then log in to access the invoice generator.

Does the site calculate totals automatically?

Yes, the site automatically calculates subtotal, tax, discount, and final total based on your entries.

Can I add multiple items to one invoice?

Yes, the invoice generator allows you to add multiple item or service rows.

Can I save, edit, and download invoices?

Yes, you can save invoices in history, edit them later, and print or download them.
